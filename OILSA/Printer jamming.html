Printer Jamming/Unable to clear Jam

Ask below questions to the user.

• What’s the problem with the device?
• When did it start?
• Is it happening now?
• Are you still able to print / copy?
• Does it occur constantly or intermittently?
• Have you tried rebooting the printer?
• Is there another printer in the area we can use to route print traffic?

We have to raise a problem ticket to fix this issue

The following information is necessary to proceed:

Device Support label on the front of the device.

a. Does the label say it’s supported by COTG (Chicago Office Technology Group) or Warehouse Direct?.

b.On the same tag displaying who supports the printer, please provide the Device ID.

Warehouse Direct is a number.

COTG is a letter and number.

*IF COTG (Chicago Office Technology Group) tag please place call to: (630) 771-8200 and document in Saint Anthony Hospital ticket.

*IF Warehouse Direct device, help desk can call up the tag number in FM software (see PrinterSupport_FM_Audit KB 408788 ), click on device, and see device status information > type tag number into the search field.

Is this an urgent issue impacting patient safety or patient care?

a. If yes to either, please open a Severity 2 ticket and follow up with hardware team on-call.

b. If no, and support tag is listed as COTG (Chicago Office Technology Group) please place call to: (630) 771-8200.

c. If no, and support tag is listed as Warehouse Direct please enter standard service calls into Warehouse Direct connect web portal. Refer to PrinterSupport_WD_Ticket KB408789.
